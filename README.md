# BEXT Arano Restoration Fix

In Career mode after the Story, the Arano Restoration is far too passive and isolated. 
They never spawn missions outside their territory, no other factions can ever target them, not even Pirates. Your reputation will forever be +100 with them.

These replacement starsystem files change that.

# Details

Major factions can now spawn missions on nearby Arano worlds.

Arano Restoration can spawn missions on nearby Independant, Taurian & Liao worlds.

Independant worlds in the Reach can now spawn missions which target Arano Restoration.

Certain Taurian, Liao, Magistracy & Davion border worlds have a chance to spawn missions targetting Arano Restoration.

Aurigan Pirates can now target Arano Restoration. They can also now spawn mission across nearly all Arano worlds.


# Bonus 

Marik is now allowed to spawn missions on Magistracy border worlds

Aurigan Pirates are now allowed to spawn missions on the small cluster of Taurian & Magistracy worlds they have a presence on.
